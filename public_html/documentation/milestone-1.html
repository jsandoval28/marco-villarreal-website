Behavioral Health Billing

Purpose, Audience & Goal

Purpose:

This project will be a professional website advertising my friend Marco's therapy billing service called Behavioral Health Billing.

Audience:

This website is designed to be found by potential clients looking for a therapy billing service in Albuquerque, New Mexico.

Goal:

The primary goal of this site is to get Marco contracting work in the field of therapy billing by advertising his service to potential clients and allowing them to initiate contact with him though a form. The secondary goal of this site is to showcase my web development abilities to potential employers.

Persona

Name: Jack Williamson

Jack Williamson is a licenced therapist in Albuquerque with his own private practice who is credentialed with a most of the insurance companies. He is looking for a therapy billing service for his practice.

Technology:
  • Jack uses a 2023 14-inch Macbook Pro with macOS Sonoma 14.2.1.
  • For mobile, he uses an iPhone 15 with iOS 17.3
  • As far as browsers go, Jack uses Safari on both his Macbook and his iPhone.
  • Jack loves technology and recognizes its necessity in today's world.
Technological Proficiency:
  • Although Jack likes to have the most up-to-date Apple technology, he is not very proficient with technology. He is an older man and regularly asks his son to help him with technical problems that arise. Jack is proficient enough, however, to browse the internet on both of his devices, to send text messages and emails, and to use the apps on his phone.

Attitudes and Behaviors:

In life and in the workspace, Jack values efficiency and artistic style. Jack is mostly laid back as a person, but nonetheless will not tolerate laziness. He gets things done skillfully and on time. He is enthusiastic about life and likes to have fun. He enjoys his work and enjoys helping people.

Frustrations and Needs:

Jack's previous therapy billing specialist quit, and now he needs to hire a new contractor with at least 2 years previous work experience at an entry-level salary. Jack wants someone who gets things done on time, does things efficiently, and styles things well. Jack also wants his new contractor to be agreeable in nature. Jack has been having a bit of trouble finding a new contractor with these qualities but needs to find one ASAP.
